## Legend

- **Name** — design name
- **URL** — Printables, Thingiverse, or repository URL
- **Author** — designer or community handle
- **Stars** — approximate likes or downloads on hosting platform
- **Last Commit** — most recent update (YYYY-MM)
- **License** — design license
- **Status** — active/stale/archived
- **Notes** — material notes, special features, or accessory compatibility
