# YouTube Channels, Podcasts & Streams

Video content and audio podcasts have been instrumental in bringing Flipper Zero to mainstream attention. This catalog covers the official Flipper Zero YouTube channel, major security/hardware-hacking channels that have produced dedicated Flipper content, viral mainstream reviews, tutorial playlists aimed at FAP developers, and security podcasts that have discussed Flipper's regulatory controversies, BadUSB capabilities, and RF research. Subscriber counts are approximate and reflect the creator's overall audience, not Flipper-specific viewership.
