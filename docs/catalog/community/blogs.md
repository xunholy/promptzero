# Blogs, Newsletters & Write-Ups

Long-form technical writing on Flipper Zero spans official product announcements, original security research disclosures, mainstream media coverage, and deep community tutorials. This catalog captures the official Flipper blog, security firm research pages whose work directly affects Flipper capabilities (e.g. the Quarkslab FM11RF08S backdoor disclosure), hacking-focused tech press, and community wiki/tutorial repositories. Entries are ordered from most active and authoritative to less-maintained resources. Where a blog covers Flipper as one of many topics, the Notes column specifies the relevant content angle.

## Legend

| Last Commit | `YYYY-MM` of most recent post or commit |
| Status | `active` = published in last 12 months; `stale` = no post in 12–24 months; `archived` = site offline/read-only |
